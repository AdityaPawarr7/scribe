What it does

Notes that write themselves,
from audio that never leaves.

🎙️

Local live transcription

whisper.cpp turns speech to text on-device while the meeting runs. The recording exists in exactly one place: your disk.

✦

Auto-notes on Stop

Hit stop and your fragments + the transcript become structured notes — summary, decisions, action items with owners. Meetings even title themselves.

💬

Ask your notes

A built-in chat over every meeting you've captured. “What did I promise last week?” — answered, with sources.

🔑

Any model, your key

One Concentrate AI key unlocks Claude, GPT, Gemini and 150+ models — switch from a dropdown. The free tier runs Scribe at $0.

📄

Your data is just files

Plain JSON + WAV on your disk. Grep them, back them up, delete them. No export button needed — nothing was taken from you.

🫧

Liquid glass, your way

Dark & light glass themes, six accent colors, four font packs. It's your desk — it should look like it.
